About us

With a huge passion for hair and trends, the doors to our exclusive hair salon The Barber opened in 1982. Our team consists of passionate and quality-conscious hairdressers. The hairdressers have broad expertise in chemical treatments, haircuts, styling, design, service and wellness.

We can guarantee that all the products we use are organic or natural. There are no artificial colors or perfumes, mineral oils, chemical preservatives, or animal ingredients. In addition, we require our suppliers to have the product shipped in the most environmentally friendly way, and that all packaging is recyclable and / or degradable.

You can find our luxurious and inviting salon in the middle of Oslo city. Welcome to us for an amazing experience.
